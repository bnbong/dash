**Badge** — small pill status label. Maps to the calm Dash status language.

```jsx
<Badge tone="positive" dot>안정적</Badge>
<Badge tone="attention" dot>회복 필요</Badge>
<Badge tone="caution" dot>과부하 주의</Badge>
<Badge tone="pine">Dash Plus</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral · pine · ochre · positive · attention · caution · calm · solid`. Use `attention` (warm), never red, for "needs recovery".
