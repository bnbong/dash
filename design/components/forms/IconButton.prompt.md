**IconButton** — icon-only control for headers, toolbars, and row affordances. Always pass a `label` for accessibility.

```jsx
<IconButton icon={<i data-lucide="settings" />} label="설정" variant="ghost" />
<IconButton icon={<i data-lucide="plus" />} label="추가" variant="soft" round />
```

Variants: `ghost` · `soft` · `solid` · `outline`. Sizes: `sm` · `md` · `lg`. Press state scales down 8%.
